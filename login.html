ABOUT US

TraveLenza is a holiday planner which helps the people to get well connected with their family. In this busy world working people find diifficult to spend the quality time with their beloved ones. We help people to plan their holidays without disturbing their busy schedule. We kicked off this start-up when one of our family member was suffering from the fear that he might lose the bond with his beloved ones. Due to his work loads he rarely spends time with them . So inorder to overcome his fear we a group of 5 came up with is idea and we completed successfully 10 years of our journey !!!

OUR SERVICES


We plan holiday trips within and outside India which is economical for common people. According to the customer's wish we estimate the total amount for the trip including food ,stay and guides .


Mexico
Mexico is famous for its vibrant culture, beautiful beaches, and rich ancient history. Mexico is also known for its delicious Mexican dishes and food, with some of its most popular exports including chocolate and hot peppers.

Australia
Australia is the largest country by area in Oceania and the world's sixth-largest country. Australia is known for many things, including swathes of tropical beaches, marine reserves, Aboriginal culture, cute koalas, rolling wine country, and lush rainforests .

Maldives


The Maldives is a tropical holiday destination in the Indian Ocean. The stunning islands are very relaxed and offer an experience which can best be described as a getaway on a desert island but with high quality accommodation, spectacular food, excellent service and an extensive range of activities.

Varkala
Varkala is a tiny hamlet on the outskirts of Thiruvananthapuram that has attracted pilgrims and tourists for a long time. Its beach is famous for its natural springs which are said to have medicinal qualities.

Goa
Goa is also called “Rome of the East” or “The Miami of India”, and has a distinct Portuguese legacy, perky nightlife, natural beauty, and great culinary experience. It is a destination for all seasons. The culture of Goan people is a blend of Portuguese and Indian culture.

Coorg
Coorg in Karnataka is known as the Scotland of India. It is also known as Kodagu, and is famous for its exquisite climate, hill ranges, and coffee plantations.
